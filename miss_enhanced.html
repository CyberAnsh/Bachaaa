## The Day You Proposed ❤️

This is one of those memories that always makes me smile whenever I think about it.

That day, I was studying. I had told you that I was going offline to focus on my studies. But because of the power cuts, I kept coming back online again and again. First I went to study, then the electricity went out. I came back online. Then the power returned, so I went back to studying. A little later the same thing happened again. It felt like the entire evening was playing a game with me.

Around 9:55 PM, while I was having dinner, you sent:

"Achaa suno ek baat bolna hai 🤡"

At that moment, I had no idea what was coming.

Since I was busy studying, I replied late. When I finally came back around 11:04 PM, I thought you were simply going to tell me whatever was on your mind.

Instead, you started with:

"Ghar hamara uss side hai"

Me:
"Kya? 🤡"

You:
"Bich ma parta hai rode 🤡"

Me:
"Mujhe kuch samjh nhi aaraha hai kya bol rahe ho"

You:
"Aab rode ka uss par hai dukan 🥸"

"Aab dukan Jana ka liya rode cross ❌ karna paraga 😌"

Me:
"Haa wo to karna padega 😶"

You:
"To aab rode kaisa cross ❌ kara 🤷🏻"

Me:
"Ithar udhar dekh lata tha 🤧"

You:
"Good good 😹"

"Kio ager gari ghoda nahi aa raha hai 😹"

"Ek daam aakh mund liya 💀"

At this point I was completely confused, but I kept following along because I wanted to know where this story was going. 😭

Then you said:

"Or dur la gye"

"Waha per ham kya karta tha"

Me:
"Wo to hai"

You:
"Kismat ka bharose jata tha 🙂"

Me:
"Aur aur"

You:
"Khatam 😁"

Me:
"Ek aur"

And then suddenly the conversation took a completely random turn.

"Heyy"

"Are you breathing"

"Tell me yes or no 😭"

"No"

Your reaction:
"✌🏻✌🏻💀💀"

"Are you gay"

Me:
"No"

You:
"I mean guy"

Me:
"Yes"

You:
"Do you think I am funny 🤣"

Me:
"Yes"

You:
"What brand is your microwave"

Me:
"I don't have any microwave"

By this point, I had completely stopped trying to guess what was happening. 😭

Then you said:

"Last question..."

And finally...

"Will you be my bf ❤️"

For a second, everything clicked.

The road.
The shop.
The random questions.
The microwave.

All of it had somehow led to this one moment.

And my reply was:

"Wo to main pahle se hi hu. ❤️"

Looking back, what I love most isn't just the question itself. It's the way you asked it. You could have asked directly, but instead you turned it into the funniest, most confusing, most unforgettable conversation ever.

And honestly, that's exactly why I love this memory so much.

It wasn't a perfect proposal.

It was your proposal.

And that's what made it perfect. ❤️✨.
